Sandwich

Description

This recipe has as many combinations as you can imagine. It is ideal for quick meals,
experimenting with flavours, or creating absolute calamities (say, peanut butter, turkey and olives)

Before you start creating your own monstruosities, we advise following
tried-and-tested versions of this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of protein (chicken, turkey, ham, etc.)
    Using readily sliced meats is a common practice and saves you time.
  • Your choice of bread (white, wheat, rye, etc. You could even go for two arepas)
  • Your choice of veggies. Lettuce leafs and sliced tomatoes are a staple,
    but consider onion, cucumber, peppers and so on.
  • Your choice sliced cheese.
  • Your choice of sauce(s) (mayo, ketchup, bbq, etc.)

Preparation

  1. Place two slices of bread next to each other on a plate.
    You can add additional layers as you grow more comfortable with the process.
  2. Spread the sauce on one of the slices, or both if you want.
  3. Start layering the veggies on one of the slices.
  4. Place the meat or protein (if vegetarian) on top of the veggies
  5. Place the cheese on top of everything
  6. Finally top the stack with the remaining slice
  7. For extra texture, toast the bread beforehand
