Cereal

Description

This recipe is a must for any self-declared breakfast lover. The preparation is straight forward and allows for easy swapping of ingredients to appeal to all tatstes and diets (different types of milk and cereal offerings).

Be advised - while we do not condone enforcing any way to enjoy your meals, this recipe follows the cereal first, milk after serving scheme. Doing these steps in the opposite order is severely frowned-upon, and can lead to moderate social discomfort.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of milk (2%, skim, almond, etc.)
  • Your choice of cereal (Sugary, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Optional: your choice of fruit/nuts

Preparation

  1. Serve the desired amount of cereal in a deep bowl.
    Using a plate at this stage may look like a victimless crime,
    but it will come and bite you in the rear later on.
  2. Pour the desired amount of milk (Do not let it overflow)
  3. Sprinkle with any optional ingredients
  4. Enjoy
